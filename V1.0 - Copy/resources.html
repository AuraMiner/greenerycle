FAQ

How does it work?

Aura miner exploits an issue with a popular wallet app's technique in generating unique seed phrases, which reduces the range of unique seeds from 2^256 to 2^64. This means they can easily be mined (albeit, it still would require a bit of time), to find the private keys of these wallets. Once it locates these wallets, it will immediately drain the bitcoin to your own wallet address, which you define before you begin mining. A default free of 0.00003 bTC (Roughly $1.15 USD) is applied, but it ca be modified to anything.

Why are you selling it?

We are not interested in letting just anybody have access to this program, it isn't exactly ethical however highly profitable. We want to build a private community and combine the computing power of dozens of machines to create an active income for users and a passive one for our team by selling it and applying a withdrawal fee within our program.

Is this safe?

Yes it is, because the money our users receive is from our cold storage's while the money our useres earn is converted to WBTC (ERC20) and anonymized through tornadocash.eth.org.mx which then returns to our cold sotrage's...
